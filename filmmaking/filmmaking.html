I hope to help create a platform that uplifts Asian American voices, including those that are underrepresented within the Asian American community. I want to celebrate the resilience, strength, and love of these different communities and preserve them through storytelling. Ultimately, the youtube channel can be whatever I want it to be; however, I want to take this opportunity to (1) celebrate the unseen stories of Asian Americans, (2) help more Asian Americans on screen through YouTube's wide-ranging audience, and (3) stimulate conversations within and around the community, much like what is being done through works such as "Gook," "Crazy Rich Asians," and "To All the Boys I've Loved Before." I hope you'll subscribe and join me on this endeavor!
